Imad Berrouyne is a postdoctoral researcher in the Interdisciplinary Centre for Security, Reliability and Trust (SnT) at the University of Luxembourg. He received a double Ph.D. degree from the Université du Québec à Chicoutimi and Institut Mines Télécom Atlantique. He developed a software engineering methodology based on Model-Driven Engineering (MDE) to overcome the heterogeneity challenges in the IoT. Before his Ph.D., Imad obtained a Bachelor degree in computer science and a Master degree in computer networking from the Université Claude Bernard Lyon 1 (Lyon, France).
